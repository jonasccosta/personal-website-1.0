Hobbies

Hiking

I started hiking at UWC-USA, the international high school I attended in New Mexico. Since my first trip, I have spent in total almost two months hiking and camping. I enjoy being in contact with nature, seeing animals in their natural environment, and the sense of community that can be constructed when hiking in a group. Personally, it is a way to release stress and find joy in the small things around me.
